FAQ

How long do huskies live?:

  • Most huskies, including different breeds of huskies live between 10-15 years.

    • Do huskies shed?:

  • YES! Huskies have long, thick coats due to being an arctic dog. They most breeds shed twice a year. You'll need to be diligent in brushing your huskies hair to minimize the amount of shedding.

    • Are huskies lazy?:

  • On the contrary, while huskies do enjoy occasional naps, cuddles and relaxation. The husky is a working dog. They have a lot of energy and prefer to be exercised regularly. This will ensure their overall happieness and health.

    • Are huskies good with kids?:

  • Each husky has its own personality and temperament depending on the breed. Some breeds of huskies might be more suitable for children than others. However, with good training any husky can be good with your children.

    • Are huskies big?:

  • Most huskies, vary in size. This also depends on the breed of husky and whether its male or female. Males tend to be bigger than females and certain breeds of huskies are bigger than others as well. However, some breeds of huskies include toys, medium and large. Make sure to do your research when choosing which breed of husky might best suit you and your families wants/needs.

    • Can I get a husky if I live in an apartment? :

  • While its not recommended, you can have a husky in an apartment if you exercise and are pro-active with allowing your pup to burn of their energy. Huskies are working dogs and are used to doing long, strenuous tasks like pulling sleds. Its very important to make sure you are exercising your pup and giving them the outlet they need. Otherwise, bad, destructive behavior can occur.
